Three locations, two windows, one promise — your camper is safe and accounted for, every moment.

  • Parents stay in the vehicle at all times
  • Hang tag on rear-view mirror
  • Carpool form on dashboard with names
Afternoon Pickup
3:30 – 4:00 PM
  • Same three locations, same routine

Policies & expectations

Code of conduct.

Clear expectations. Consistent enforcement. A safe, joyful camp for everyone.

1

Camper expectations

▼
  • Respect staff, fellow campers, and camp property.
  • Follow instructions from counselors and unit leaders.
  • Stay with your group — never wander off.
  • Keep hands & words kind. No bullying.
  • Camper cellphones not permitted during camp.
  • No fireworks, weapons, or toy weapons.
  • No drugs, tobacco, alcohol, or intoxicants.
  • Inclusion matters — everyone in.
2

Zero tolerance AD.16

▼
  • No corporal punishment — ever.
  • No illegal drugs, underage drinking, or weapons.
  • No smoking or vaping on MARJCC property.
  • No personal animals (service animals welcome under ADA).
3

Behavior support AD.13

▼

When a camper struggles, our trained staff respond with patience first. If patterns persist, our behavioral specialist conducts an observation and oversees a behavior plan with staff, you, and your child — together.

4

Photo & video release

▼

By signing registration, you grant MARJCC the right to photograph campers and use the images in publicity and web content. To opt out, notify Camp Reception in writing before camp begins.

5

Activities & trips AD.33.2

▼

By e-signing the registration form, parents/guardians permit their child to participate in all camp activities, including planned and unplanned trips using different modes of transportation.
